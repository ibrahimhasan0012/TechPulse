    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does the auto-mode work?

    Just switch it on and stand it up. The built-in UV light turns on to attract mosquitoes in the dark, and the electric grid zaps them automatically.

    Is it safe to leave around kids or pets?

    Yes, the swatter has a triple-layer safety grid. The outer meshes do not carry a charge, preventing accidental shocks if brushed against.

    Do I need to buy batteries for it?

    No! It comes with a built-in 1200mAh rechargeable lithium battery that easily tops up using a standard USB Type-C cable.

    Can I use it outdoors?

    Absolutely. It is lightweight and portable, making it perfect for clearing out mosquitoes on your patio, balcony, or while camping.
